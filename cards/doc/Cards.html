    deal(deck, hand_size)

    Divides a deck into a hand and the remainder of the deck. The hand_size argument indicates how many cards should be in the hand.

    ## Examples

    iex> deck = Cards.create_deck()
iex> {hand, deck} = Cards.deal(deck, 1)
iex> hand
["Ace of Spades"]
